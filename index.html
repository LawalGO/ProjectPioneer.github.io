Diabetes Prediction

This Diabetes Prediction Project aimed to develop a robust predictive model to identify individuals at risk of developing diabetes based on various health and lifestyle factors. Utilizing a comprehensive dataset obtained from a healthcare institution, I cleaned the dataset, perform exploratory analysis, and applied machine learning algorithms. The Random Forest Model with standadization seems to perform better than other models. ultimately achieving an accuracy rate of 97% with a gradient boosting classifier.

Computer Vision Pose Detection

This Pose Detection project focused on leveraging computer vision techniques to accurately identify and analyze human body postures in real-time. Utilizing deep learning models, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), I developed a system that processes video feeds to detect key body landmarks, enabling applications in fitness tracking and gesture recognition. By implementing data augmentation techniques and fine-tuning pre-trained models, the project achieved a high accuracy in pose estimation, showcasing the potential of computer vision to enhance human-computer interaction and promote active lifestyles.

  • Unveiling Motion Dynamics

Computer vision Hand Detection Project

This Hand Detection project employed advanced computer vision techniques to accurately locate and recognize human hands in various environments. Utilizing deep learning models, specifically convolutional neural networks (CNNs), I developed a system that processed video streams to identify hand gestures and positions in real-time. By training the model on diverse datasets and implementing data augmentation strategies, the project achieved high precision in hand detection, enabling potential applications in human-computer interaction, sign language recognition, and augmented reality. This work highlights the transformative role of computer vision in enhancing intuitive user experiences and accessibility.
